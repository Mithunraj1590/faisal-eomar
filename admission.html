Admission Process & Eligibility

Join Fazl E Omar Public School - Your journey to excellence begins here

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age Requirements: Students must meet the age criteria as per CBSE guidelines for the respective class.
  • Previous School Records: Transfer certificate and previous academic records are required for admission.
  • Medical Certificate: A valid medical certificate is mandatory for all new admissions.
  • Birth Certificate: Original birth certificate must be submitted during admission.
  • Passport Size Photos: 4 recent passport size photographs of the student.

Admission Process

Step 1: Application Submission

Fill out the admission form available online or collect from the school office. Submit along with required documents.

Step 2: Document Verification

Our admission team will verify all submitted documents and eligibility criteria.

Step 3: Fee Payment

Pay the registration fee of ₹2000 (UKG to 1st std exempted) and complete the admission formalities.

Step 4: Confirmation

Receive admission confirmation and orientation details for the new academic year.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from parents about admissions

The age requirement follows CBSE guidelines. For UKG, children should be 4.5 to 5.5 years old. For Class 1, children should be 5.5 to 6.5 years old. Age requirements increase by one year for each subsequent class. Please refer to the CBSE age criteria or contact our admission office for specific age requirements.

Required documents include: (1) Original birth certificate, (2) Transfer certificate from previous school (if applicable), (3) Previous academic records/mark sheets, (4) Medical certificate, (5) 4 recent passport size photographs, (6) Aadhaar card copy of student and parents, (7) Address proof, and (8) Caste certificate (if applicable).

The registration fee is ₹2000 for new admissions. However, UKG to 1st standard students are exempted from this registration fee. The annual fee structure varies by class and includes tuition fees, development fees, and other charges. Please download the fee structure document or contact the school office for detailed fee information.

For lower classes (UKG to Class 5), admission is generally based on document verification and availability of seats. For higher classes (Class 6 onwards), there may be an assessment or interaction with the student to evaluate their academic level. The specific process depends on the class and seat availability.

School timings are from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Saturday is a half-day with classes from 9:00 AM to 12:30 PM. The school follows the CBSE academic calendar with appropriate holidays and breaks.

Yes, the school provides transportation facilities. School buses cover various routes in and around Kannur. The transportation fee is separate from the tuition fee and varies based on the distance. Please contact the school office for route details and transportation fees.

Fazl E Omar Public School provides modern facilities including well-equipped classrooms, science and computer labs, library, sports facilities, playground, auditorium, canteen, medical room, and transportation. The school also offers various extracurricular activities, sports programs, and scholarship opportunities for meritorious students.

After submitting your admission application, you will receive an acknowledgment with a reference number. You can track your application status by calling our admission helpline at +91-99610 84236 or emailing us at fazleomar@gmail.com with your reference number. Our admission team will keep you updated on the status of your application.
