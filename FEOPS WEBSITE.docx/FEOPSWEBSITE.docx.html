1. Home Page: Short introduction about the school, key highlights, banner tagline(s)

Fazl-e-Omar public School was established the year 1992 at Koyyodanchal in Koodali Panchayat under Fazle e Omar trust. The main objective behind opening of a CBSE school in Koodali is to make accessible modern education in rural areas and to work intensively for the educational need of the weaker sections and prepare students to become useful responsible citizens of our motherland.

It started with a small strength of thirty-nine students in 1992. A small building with two rooms was the beginning of our education pioneer. Principal Marina Devaraj assisted by Ambika Selva Rani and attender Celine have very important role in Our educational journey. First school management formed with chairman was U Mansoor Ahmed and administrator A P Anwar Ahmed.

In 1993 School had made unbelievable progress with the student strength above hundred.

As the reward for our hard work and dedication in 1994 we had our own school building, at koyyodanchal koodali with strength of 160 wards. We were fortunate, our school building was inaugurated by honourable minister N Ramakrishnan. Indeed, to say a very strong ambitious management team worked a lot for fulfilling our dream.

It gained CBSE affiliation in 2002, making it one among the Kannur’s oldest private CBSE school. The institution has a proud track record of 100% success in public examinations, with about 75% of students achieving first-class marks.

In 2002, we shifted class 1 to 9 in a new building at Benganaparamba adorned with scenic beauty and enchanting atmosphere with lush green environment. In 2004, the first batch of students appeared AISSE class 10 board examination and obtained a remarkable result (100%). Presently 18 batches of class 10 students passed out from our school with 100% result. Fazl-e-Omar public school, Koodali always shines with its academic excellence and glory of its result in AISSE with all years (2004-2025).

In addition to academics, the school has a strong emphasis on sports, with students receiving scholarships from CBSE, and several winning the NCERT Talent Search Scholarship.
The school aims to provide a modern educational experience, focusing on holistic development, character building, and fostering close teacher-student relationships. It incorporates elements from English public-school traditions that resonate with Indian values.

Our Vision

3. Academics: Curriculum details, subjects, teaching methodology, academic calendar

CURRICULUM

Our curriculum emphasizes a holistic approach with a focus on developing 21st-century skills and includes co-scholastic areas such as Health and Physical Education, and Art Education. 

Our curriculum is entirely based on national educational framework developed by the Central Board of Secondary Education for schools in India and abroad. It focuses on core subjects like science, math, and social science, uses NCERT textbooks as a base. The curriculum is designed to build a strong academic foundation while also incorporating life skills, values, and a focus on holistic development. 

Key features of the curriculum

  • Holistic development: 

The curriculum is designed to promote the overall development of students, including physical, cognitive, emotional, and social aspects. 

  • 21st-century skills: 

There is an increased emphasis on developing skills like critical thinking, creativity, and communication through subjects like computer applications. 

  • Practical application: 

The curriculum encourages the application of knowledge in real-life contexts.

TEACHING METHODOLOGY

Our school use a variety of innovative teaching methodologies, moving away from traditional lecture-based formats to more interactive and learner-centric approaches. These methods include project-based learning, inquiry-based learning, flipped classrooms, experiential learning, collaborative learning, and the blended learning model, which combines face-to-face instruction with digital resources. These techniques aim to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and life skills while making learning more engaging and effective.
